# Intro: Why Now

> Three independent trends are converging at the same moment.

Great infrastructure opportunities are rarely created by any single trend — they are created by **the convergence of several**. The commercialization of the internet was the result of broadband, the browser, and online credit-card payment all maturing at once; the explosion of the smartphone was the confluence of the touchscreen, mobile networks, and the app store.

Today, on the payments track, three trends are converging:

1. **Stablecoins are becoming the global payment layer** — on-chain settlement volume has already surpassed the combined total of the traditional card networks.
2. **PayFi is bringing the time value of money on-chain** — a new paradigm that pulls real cash flow into the crypto world.
3. **The AI-agent economy is arriving** — a brand-new, non-human payment actor is rising.

And at the intersection of these three trends lies a **whitespace no one yet occupies**: no blockchain has been designed from the foundation for "PayFi + AI-agent payments."

This part unpacks these three trends one by one, and the opportunity where they converge:

* **2.4** The new payment actor: the rise of the AI-agent economy.
* **2.5** Who is on this track, and their paths and limits.
* **2.6** Where the whitespace is, and just how large this market really is.
