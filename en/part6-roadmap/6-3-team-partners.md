## A team assembled for "honesty"

This whitepaper stresses "honesty" throughout — honesty about performance numbers ([3.3](../part3-architecture/3-3-consensus-finality.md)), honesty about the limits of AI's capabilities ([5.4](../part5-ai/5-4-honest-ai.md)), honesty about the progress of decentralization ([6.2](6-2-governance.md)). That honesty ultimately has to be delivered by people.

The AXON team believes: **in an industry saturated with over-promising, long-termism and honesty are themselves the greatest competitive advantage.** Building an L1 born for payments is something that takes years, engineering discipline, and compliance patience. We choose to explain it clearly and build it solidly, rather than tell it prettily and build it sloppily.
