# Part VII · Tokenomics

> Value capture = fee revenue buys back and burns. An economic design that mints real business growth into token value.

The first six parts made clear what AXON is, where the market is, how the foundation is built, how PayFi lands, how AI plugs in, and how the roadmap unfolds. This part answers the final question: **where does the value of the AXON token come from.**

AXON's answer is one sentence: **not from inflationary subsidies, but from real business.** Every payment, settlement, credit, and AI-agent call the network carries generates real protocol fee revenue at the chain layer; that revenue buys back AXON proportionally and burns it, translating the network's real growth into the token's scarcity and value. This is of a piece with [3.1 Value settles at the chain layer](../part3-architecture/3-1-why-own-l1.md).
