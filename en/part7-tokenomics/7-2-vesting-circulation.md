# 7.2 Vesting & Circulating Supply

## Vesting rules per bucket

At TGE only a very small portion is released; the rest unlocks per each bucket's independent lockup and linear-release rules:

| Bucket | Vesting rule |
| --- | --- |
| Ecosystem | Multi-domain ecosystem unlocks, multi-chain weighted allocation |
| Team & Advisors | 1-year lock + 3-year linear |
| Angel | 2-year lock + 3-year linear (5 years total) |
| Strategic (private) | 12-month cliff + 24-month linear |
| Airdrop | TGE 25% + quarterly linear |
| Liquidity / Market-Making | Listing liquidity locked 6 months; base pool unlocked |

The core intent of this design is to **bind the interests of early capital and the team to the network's long-term maturation** — Angel at 5 years total, Team at 4 years (1-year lock + 3-year linear), Strategic at 3 years — all lockup periods markedly longer than industry convention.

## Circulating-release cadence

At TGE only a small portion enters circulation; the rest unlocks per the bucket lockup and linear-release rules in the table above, smoothly reaching full circulation over roughly 5 years (60 months). **The specific TGE float ratio and the month-by-month circulating curve are not disclosed at this time.**

The combination of **low initial float + long linear release** means the float on the market at TGE is very thin, easing early sell pressure; while the full 5-year release period leaves the network a time window to cash "real business growth" into "token value" — which is exactly where the [7.3 Deflationary Flywheel](7-3-utility-flywheel.md) comes into play.
