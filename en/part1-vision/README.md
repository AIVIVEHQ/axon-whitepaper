# Intro: Foundation & Wedge

> Money is going digital, but the rails that carry it have not kept up.

Every leap in financial infrastructure begins with a plain observation: **the way value moves has changed, while the underlying rails are still stuck in the previous era.** The nineteenth-century telegraph reshaped information but never reshaped clearing; twentieth-century SWIFT unified messaging but never unified settlement; today, stablecoins let "one dollar of value" cross the planet in seconds — yet they still run on rails built for general-purpose compute and improvised into carrying payments.

AXON Finance starts from a single ambition: to lay, for this new flow of value, a rail **designed for it from the foundation up**.

This part answers three questions:

* **1.1** What is our core thesis? — "The L1 is the foundation, PayFi is the wedge," and the business logic behind it.
* **1.2** What exactly is AXON Finance? — the full picture of a high-performance, AI-native PayFi blockchain.
* **1.3** What justifies this design? — the first principles underpinning every technical choice.

By the end of Part I, you will understand that AXON is not "yet another faster chain," but a foundation-level judgment about **payments, settlement, and the machine economy**.
